Why Donate?

Blood is the most precious gift. Your donation can save up to three lives and there's no substitute for human blood.

Who Can Donate?

Most people in good health, at least 17 years old, and weighing at least 110 pounds can donate blood.

The Process

The entire process takes about an hour. The actual donation only takes 8-10 minutes. It's safe and simple.
